Diseases We Detect

🦷

Calculus (Tartar)

Hardened plaque buildup on teeth that can cause gum disease and tooth decay. Early detection helps prevent serious complications.

⚫

Caries (Cavities)

Tooth decay caused by bacterial acids. Our AI can detect cavities in early stages before they become severe.

😐

Hypodontia

Congenital condition with missing teeth. Accurate detection assists in treatment planning and orthodontic management.

🟡

Tooth Discoloration

Staining or discoloration of teeth due to various causes. Helps identify underlying conditions or treatment needs.

🔴

Mouth Ulcer

Oral sores or ulceration in the mouth. Early detection can prevent infection and guide proper treatment.
